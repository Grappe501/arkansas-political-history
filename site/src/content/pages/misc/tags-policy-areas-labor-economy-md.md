\# Labor & Economy

\## Definition

This policy area includes laws and practices affecting work, wages, workplace conditions, and economic development.

---

\## Why It Matters

Labor policy determines:

\- who benefits from economic growth,

\- how risk is distributed,

\- and whether workers have collective power.

---

\## Arkansas Context

Arkansas has historically emphasized:

\- low labor regulation,

\- agricultural and extractive industries,

\- and centralized economic decision-making.

These choices shaped regional inequality.
