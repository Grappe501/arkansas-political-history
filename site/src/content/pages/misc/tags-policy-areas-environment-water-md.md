\# Environment & Water

\## Definition

This policy area covers land use, water protection, environmental regulation, and resource management.

---

\## Why It Matters

Environmental decisions affect:

\- public health,

\- economic sustainability,

\- and long-term community viability.

Impacts often fall unevenly.

---

\## Arkansas Context

Environmental governance in Arkansas reflects:

\- agricultural priorities,

\- regulatory exemptions,

\- limited enforcement capacity,

\- and local resistance movements.

---

\## Common Power Mechanisms

\- Regulatory Carveouts

\- Enforcement Weakening

\- Narrative Legitimation
