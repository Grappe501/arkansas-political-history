# Roles & Responsibilities

This archive uses a **layered contribution model** to balance openness with rigor.

---

## 1. Public Contributors

Who:
- Researchers
- Journalists
- Community members
- Historians
- Students

What they can do:
- Suggest sources
- Submit corrections
- Propose new pages or edits
- Flag errors or omissions

Public contributors do not publish directly to the archive.

---

## 2. Editorial Stewards

Who:
- Designated by Stand Up Arkansas
- Selected for subject-matter competence
- Trained in archive standards

Responsibilities:
- Review submissions
- Verify sources
- Apply tagging and standards
- Draft or revise content
- Document editorial decisions

Stewards are accountable to governance standards, not ideology.

---

## 3. Archive Maintainers

Who:
- Stand Up Arkansas project leads
- Technical and editorial leads

Responsibilities:
- Maintain structure and standards
- Resolve disputes when necessary
- Approve major framework changes
- Ensure long-term continuity

Maintainers do not override evidence.
They ensure process integrity.
