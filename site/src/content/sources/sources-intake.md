\# How Sources Enter the Archive

Sources are added through:

\- archival research,

\- public records review,

\- academic work,

\- community documentation.

---

\## Intake Process (Overview)

1\. Source identified

2\. Source categorized

3\. Relevant claims extracted

4\. Claims linked to source

5\. Context and limitations noted

6\. Source preserved or referenced

---

\## Public Contributions

Future versions of this archive may allow public source submissions.

All submissions are reviewed before inclusion.

---

\## Why This Matters

An archive that grows without discipline loses trust.

This system grows deliberately.
