\# Education

\## Definition

This policy area covers public education governance, funding, access, and accountability.

---

\## Why It Matters

Education policy shapes:

\- economic opportunity,

\- civic participation,

\- and intergenerational mobility.

Decisions about funding and control have long-term consequences.

---

\## Arkansas Context

Education entered the Arkansas Constitution during Reconstruction.

Subsequent eras saw:

\- segregation and unequal funding,

\- centralization and decentralization struggles,

\- ongoing litigation over adequacy and equity.

---

\## Common Power Mechanisms

\- Funding Withdrawal

\- Preemption

\- Executive Weakening

\- Judicial Delay
