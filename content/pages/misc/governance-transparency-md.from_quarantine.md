\# Transparency & Accountability

This archive is accountable to the public.

---

\## What Is Publicly Visible

\- Page status (draft, active)

\- Version history

\- Source lists

\- Update dates

\- Governance framework

---

\## What Is Not Public (for safety and integrity)

\- Personal contact information

\- Internal review notes

\- Harassment mitigation processes

---

\## Public Feedback

Readers may:

\- submit corrections,

\- challenge interpretations,

\- suggest additions.

All feedback is reviewed and logged.

---

\## Why This Matters

Transparency is how trust is earned and maintained.
