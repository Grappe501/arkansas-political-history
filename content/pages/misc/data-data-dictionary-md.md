\## Purpose

This document defines all quantitative indicators used in the archive, including:

\- what each indicator measures,

\- why it matters,

\- how it should be interpreted,

\- and where it comes from.

Indicators are descriptive, not explanatory.

They support analysis; they do not replace it.

---

\## Core Indicator Principles

\- Each indicator must have a clear definition.

\- Indicators must be traceable to an official source.

\- Changes over time must reference consistent methodology.

\- Limitations must be noted.

---

\## Census Indicators (ACS / Decennial)

\### total_population

\*\*Definition:\*\* Total resident population of a place.

\*\*Source:\*\* U.S. Census Bureau (Decennial / ACS 5-year)

\*\*Why it matters:\*\* Baseline for representation, services, and resource allocation.

\*\*Notes:\*\* Decennial preferred for long-term comparisons.

---

\### race_ethnicity_distribution

\*\*Definition:\*\* Population by race and ethnicity categories.

\*\*Source:\*\* U.S. Census Bureau (ACS 5-year)

\*\*Why it matters:\*\* Essential for understanding representation, dilution, and exclusion.

\*\*Notes:\*\* Categories follow Census definitions for the given vintage.

---

\### median_household_income

\*\*Definition:\*\* Median income of households in a place.

\*\*Source:\*\* U.S. Census Bureau (ACS 5-year)

\*\*Why it matters:\*\* Proxy for economic stability and opportunity.

\*\*Notes:\*\* Inflation-adjusted values must specify base year.

---

\### poverty_rate

\*\*Definition:\*\* Percentage of population below federal poverty threshold.

\*\*Source:\*\* U.S. Census Bureau (ACS 5-year)

\*\*Why it matters:\*\* Measures economic vulnerability and policy impact.

\*\*Notes:\*\* Threshold definitions vary by household composition.

---

\### educational_attainment

\*\*Definition:\*\* Percentage of adults with HS diploma or higher; BA or higher.

\*\*Source:\*\* U.S. Census Bureau (ACS 5-year)

\*\*Why it matters:\*\* Long-term civic and economic outcomes.

\*\*Notes:\*\* Age thresholds must be specified (typically 25+).

---

\### housing_tenure

\*\*Definition:\*\* Owner-occupied vs renter-occupied housing units.

\*\*Source:\*\* U.S. Census Bureau (ACS 5-year)

\*\*Why it matters:\*\* Stability, displacement risk, and wealth building.

\*\*Notes:\*\* Often correlated with policy and zoning decisions.

---

\## BLS Indicators

\### unemployment_rate

\*\*Definition:\*\* Percentage of labor force unemployed.

\*\*Source:\*\* BLS Local Area Unemployment Statistics (LAUS)

\*\*Why it matters:\*\* Short-term economic health indicator.

\*\*Notes:\*\* Monthly series; annual averages recommended for comparison.

---

\### labor_force

\*\*Definition:\*\* Number of employed + unemployed persons actively participating.

\*\*Source:\*\* BLS LAUS

\*\*Why it matters:\*\* Contextualizes unemployment rates.

\*\*Notes:\*\* Changes may reflect participation shifts, not job creation.

---

\### employment_by_industry

\*\*Definition:\*\* Employment counts by NAICS industry.

\*\*Source:\*\* BLS QCEW

\*\*Why it matters:\*\* Economic structure and vulnerability.

\*\*Notes:\*\* Suppression may occur for confidentiality.

---

\## Elections Indicators

\### votes_cast

\*\*Definition:\*\* Total ballots cast in a race.

\*\*Source:\*\* Arkansas Secretary of State / OpenElections

\*\*Why it matters:\*\* Participation and engagement proxy.

\*\*Notes:\*\* Does not equal turnout without registration denominator.

---

\### vote_share

\*\*Definition:\*\* Percentage of votes received by a candidate.

\*\*Source:\*\* Arkansas Secretary of State / OpenElections

\*\*Why it matters:\*\* Measures competitiveness and dominance.

\*\*Notes:\*\* Must specify race and jurisdiction.

---

\### election_type

\*\*Definition:\*\* General, primary, runoff, special.

\*\*Source:\*\* Arkansas Secretary of State

\*\*Why it matters:\*\* Turnout and participation vary significantly by type.
